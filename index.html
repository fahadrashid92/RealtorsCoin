Frequently asked questions

What is ICO ?

An initial coin offering or initial currency offering is a type of funding using cryptocurrencies. It is often a form of crowdfunding, although a private ICO which does not seek public investment is also possible.

Are ICO a good Investment?

They offer high potential profits if you can determine which cryptocurrency is a good investment. Since you're buying early, prices are often lower, and some ICOs offer tokens at discounted rates. ICOs are accessible to anyone. Unlike some IPOs, there aren't any restrictions on who can invest.

What is Cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency, crypto-currency, or crypto is a collection of binary data which is designed to work as a medium of exchange.

What is cryptocurrency in Real Estate?

When a real-world asset, like a house, is funded through cryptocurrency, its value is converted into security tokens that prove each holder's fraction of ownership in the asset. Investors can purchase these tokens during the ICO period to crowdfund a house flip or other investment purchase.

What is Crypto Exchange Listing?

A crypto exchange is similar to an online stock market for cryptocurrencies. These exchanges provide investment choices for traders who buy, sell, or hold digital assets such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin.

What is DeFi?

Decentralized Finance is a blockchain-based form of finance that does not rely on central financial intermediaries such as brokerages, exchanges, or banks to offer traditional financial instruments, and instead utilizes smart contracts on blockchains, the most common being Ethereum.
